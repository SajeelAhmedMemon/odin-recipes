Biryani

Delicious sindhi style biryani.

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  1. Wash & soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil rice with salt + whole spices until 70% cooked, then drain.
  3. Fry sliced onions in oil until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, then chicken. Cook till color changes.
  5. Add salt, spices and yogurt. Cook till chicken is tender.
  6. Cover & cook on low heat (dum) for 10 to 15 minutes.
