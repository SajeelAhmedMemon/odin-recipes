Chicken Curry

A simple, spicy dish made with chicken cooked in onion-tomato gravy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Fry onion in oil till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add chicken and spices.
  4. Add tomato and water.
  5. Cook till chicken is tender.
